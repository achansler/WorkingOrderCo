WORKING ORDER is a wellness company founded in Brooklyn, NY.

We create earthly pleasures—delicious foods, alluring objects, and sensuous spaces—inspired by nature.

Everything we do is designed to help us enjoy a connection to the restorative powers of the natural world so that we can look and feel our best.

There are three core ideas that guide us.

Nature knows best

Nature already contains everything we need to feel our best inside and out—so it’s where we begin in everything that we create.

Wellness Should Be Enjoyed, Not Endured

Our paths towards well-being shouldn't have to come with more sacrifice and judgement. Instead, our version of wellness embraces more ease, more wonder, and more pleasure.

“In all things of nature there is something of the marvelous”
Aristotle

Downtime Is Not Wasted Time

Our modern life deserves more moments of quiet contemplation. These small moments have big potential to restore us.
